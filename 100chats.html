100 Chats for Studying, Career & College Life

OpenAI brought together 70 college students from across the U.S. and Canada to share their most useful ChatGPT conversations, meeting weekly to show their best chats and surface what actually works. I was part of that contributor group.

Over months of testing, the top 100 prompts were compiled into a printed book and distributed on campuses nationwide.

The book was a limited-edition print run handed out at 50+ colleges. The full collection lives at chatgpt.com.
