I'm a UCLA student. Here's how I get the most out of OpenAI's new Atlas browser.
October 24, 2025

Got early access to ChatGPT Atlas — OpenAI's AI-native browser that merges ChatGPT with a web browser and its Agents system — and wrote about how I actually use it as a student.

"My favorite feature is being able to open lectures directly in Canvas and use the Ask ChatGPT button without having to download large PDFs. It gives ChatGPT full context about what my professor is teaching. It feels like having a personal tutor."

I use Atlas to quiz myself on unlabeled diagrams for my physiological science class — opening lab images and asking ChatGPT to test me on cranial and spinal nerve diagrams in real time.
